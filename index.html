Personal Reflections

Our Views

A personal reflections of an individual about the impact of numerical analysis in engineering and science.

Abnormal Individual:


When I first encountering the numerical analysis, I thought it was just approximation that is useful for financing, size measurement, but I was wrong. But after learning a bit of numerical analysis, I realized that it's a hidden mechanism behind modern science and technology. My professor told us that an exact number is not always an correct answer, sometimes just a bit of approximation can make things much smoother, for example in soil volume measurement, numerical analysis plays an important role here as it understands the failure mechanism of soil-structure interactions, they take the soil's behavior under various loading conditions to predict how structure will perform in actual scenarios.

So, numerical analysis just changed how I view precision: even just a small amount of change in soil volume can be devastating, but studying it can prevent these small error that can cause bigger problems later on.

Central Tendency

On the other hand, this image displays the mean, median, mode which is called central tendency, this is used to describe the center of a dataset. In numerical analysis this method is used to summarize large amounts of numbers in a single representative, making it easier to interpret and compare the results.
